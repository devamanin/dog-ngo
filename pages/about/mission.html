Mission & Vision
NGO is a registered public charity that operates a lifesaving animal welfare organization. Since 1998 we have worked diligently to enhance the lives of animals in the India and overseas through the programs directed at education, adoption, foster, rescue and affordable veterniary

NGO's goals are to:
  • Reduce pet overpopulation in the community
  • Prevent cruelty to all animals
  • Promote respect and comparison for all animals
  • Provide temporary shelter and care for homeless and unwanted animals
  • Find Loving homes for the animals in our care
NGO is non-profit animal welfare organization established in 2022 to end the unnecessary killing of companion animals.

All donations are tax deductible to the fullest extent of the law.
